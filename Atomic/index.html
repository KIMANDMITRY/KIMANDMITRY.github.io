ABOUT ATOMIC NETWORK

Atomic Network is boldly going where no streaming entertainment network has gone before.

We’re blowing out the tired, traditional way of making and watching television. Instead, e’re creating a revolutionary new bscriberviewer experience, forever-changing the old-school Hollywood network delivery system.

This isn’t your father’s TV network. This is the Future of television entertainment. Atomic Network will never dumb down your entertainment programming. This is Smart TV, featuring an exciting variety of original, exclusive, content. From intense sci-fi dramas to augh-out-loud comedies and provocative science and technology documentaries. Atomic Network is created by fans FOR fans. TV for Trekkies, Techies, Geeks, and Gamers.
