Driver rates

ABEE is a rideshare platform enabling drivers to keep 95% of everything they make while driving. This is 75% less than other major platforms. ABEE uses the 5% to make sure the platform is robust and stable ensuring drivers a quality experience while they run their own rideshare service.

on the blockchain

As ABEE is a hybrid platform we take every opportunity to keep a balanced approach. ABEE is a combination of decentralized and centralized functions. What this means is that in the beginning ABEE must take platform governance control in regards to server integration and network stability so that platform users can have a seamless experience, however ABEEs long term mission is to apply more decentralized features as we integrate our platform into the QTUM network.

tokens

The ABEE token has a strong utility to drivers, drivers holding 100,000 ABEE tokens will have the ability to give up to 50 of their passengers per month a 17% discount on every ride the driver chooses while still earning the full fare. This special program is unique to ABEE and comes out of ABEEs pocket reinforcing our selfless outlook on business and showing our commitment to giving drivers generous benefits.

Our mission

The ABEE platform allows for the power of the rideshare business to remain largely between the common people as it was created for them; drivers get to keep more of their earnings and they can create their own rideshare network, providing tools and advanced features for drivers to take the rideshare industry and culture to the next level. Passengers benefit as well, not only are they given incentives to ride with ABEE drivers, they are also provided with plenty of options so they can have control over how and when they want to ride.

why ride with abee?

As a rider you will be exposed to many features that are native to the ABEE platform. The mission of ABEE in terms of riders is to provide them with options, benefits and a truly peer to peer experience. Riders can choose a driver thats close to them on the map, favorite their best driver, Get an ABEE ride off the street and even earn while getting a ride.

ABEE riders pay less for rides

A combination of factors allow riders to save. Since Drivers on the ABEE platform keep more of what they earn ABEE is able to undercut other ride share platforms and charge less for rides, add in the ability for riders to earn ABEE tokens by verifying the drivers in vehicle advertisements, while taking into account drivers being able to give 17% discounts on rides and the end result is a cheaper ride with a happier driver who is making more, creating a win-win situation and allowing for a true peer to peer transaction to take place.
